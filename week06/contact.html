Visitor Tips

  • Arrive Early: To avoid large crowds, especially on weekends, try to visit popular attractions in the morning.
  • Use Public Transport: Parking can be difficult. The city offers special bus routes during the fair.
  • Stay Hydrated: Aguascalientes can be warm. Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
